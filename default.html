About Us

Aditya Foundation's Nasha Mukti Kendra is a beacon of hope for individuals struggling with addiction. With a mission to provide compassionate care and evidence-based treatments, our center is dedicated to helping individuals break free from the cycle of substance abuse and reclaim their lives.

Our team of experienced professionals offers personalized care, holistic healing, and ongoing support to empower individuals on their journey to recovery. At Aditya Foundation, we believe in treating each person with dignity, respect, and confidentiality, creating a safe and nurturing environment for healing and growth. If you or a loved one is battling addiction, reach out to us today and take the first step towards a brighter future.
